## Learning: The Algebra

The keys $\textsf{sk}$ (secret key) and $\textsf{pk}$ (public key):

$$
\textsf{sk}: \textbf{s} \leftarrow [\beta]^m,
\textsf{pk}: (\textbf{A} \leftarrow \mathbb{Z}^{m \times m}_q,\ \textbf{t} = \textbf{As}) 
$$

The encryption of a message $\mu$ to produce the ciphertext $(\textbf{u}, v)$ is
(where $r \leftarrow [\beta]^m$):

$$
(\textbf{u}^T = \textbf{r}^T \textbf{A},\ v = \textbf{r}^T\textbf{t} + \mu)
$$

Decryption is then:

$$
\mu = v - \textbf{u}^T\textbf{s}
$$
