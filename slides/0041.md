## Learning: The Algebra

<span class="hl">This works because:</span>

$$
\begin{align}
\mu &= v - \textbf{u}^T\textbf{s} \\
    &= (\textbf{r}^T\textbf{t} + \mu) - \textbf{u}^T\textbf{s} \\
    &= (\textbf{r}^T\textbf{t} + \mu) - \textbf{r}^T\textbf{A}s \\
    &= (\textbf{r}^T\textbf{As} + \mu) - \textbf{r}^T\textbf{A}s \\
    &= (\cancel{\textbf{r}^T\textbf{As}} + \mu) - \cancel{\textbf{r}^T\textbf{A}s} \\
    &= \mu
\end{align}
$$
