## What's Wrong with Classical Cryptography?

Asymmetric cryptography that is based on factoring is susceptible to quantum
attack.

Cracking an asymmetric exchange means the symmetric key that was shared between
parties is leaked.

This means that _all_ encrypted traffic between those parties can be read as if
it's plaintext.
