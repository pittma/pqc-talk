## Learning _with_ Errors

The keys $\textsf{sk}$ (secret key) and $\textsf{pk}$ (public key):

$$
\textsf{sk}: \textbf{s} \leftarrow [\beta]^m,
\textsf{pk}: (\textbf{A} \leftarrow \mathbb{Z}^{m \times m}_q,\ \textbf{t} = \textbf{As} \textcolor{red}{+ \textbf{e}})
$$

$\textcolor{red}{\textbf{e}}$ adds noise. After reduction, instead of just $\mu$,
we end up with:

$$
e + \frac{q}{2} \mu
$$

We can then determine $\mu$ as $0$ or $1$, by determining if the above result
is closer to $0$ or $q/2$.
